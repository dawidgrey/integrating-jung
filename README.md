## About the Book

"Integrating Jung" is a practical guide to Jungian psychology, covering 35 archetypes across the full spectrum of human psychological experience — from foundational patterns like the Shadow and the Hero to advanced archetypes like the Devouring Mother and the Visionary.

Written for people who have better things to do than read twenty volumes of Swiss psychoanalytic theory.
